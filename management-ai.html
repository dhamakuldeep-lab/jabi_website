AI for Management

A practical program showing future managers how to use AI for better decisions - in analytics, marketing, finance and strategy - no technical background needed.

Book this Program

Why this program

Managers don't need to build AI - but they must know how to use it and lead teams that do. This workshop gives management students a confident, practical grasp of AI in business.

What we cover

Data-driven decision making

How to read, question and act on data and AI outputs - and avoid common pitfalls.

AI in marketing and forecasting

Customer segmentation, demand forecasting, personalisation and campaign optimisation using AI.

AI in finance and operations

Risk analysis, fraud detection and process automation in a business context.

Hands-on business case

Participants work through a real business case using AI tools and present recommendations.

What participants achieve

  • Speak confidently about AI in a business setting
  • Use AI tools for a real business case
  • Understand how to lead data-driven teams
  • Receive a certificate and a verifiable experience letter
