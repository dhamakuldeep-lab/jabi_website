Not theory from a textbook — AI taught by someone who shipped it at scale.

Jabi Intelligents is founded by Kuldeep Dhama, who brings 26 years in software and AI in corporate leadership roles, including 15 years working onsite in the USA. A Computer Science & Engineering graduate (B.E.) who has also completed an Executive Leadership Program at Harvard University (Boston, USA), he has held senior positions — Head of Research & Development (Data Science), Country Head and Chief Architect — and designed and successfully implemented AI platforms and machine-learning models in healthcare that today serve millions of members.

Through Jabi Intelligents, he now brings that real-world experience directly to students — running workshops, tech talks, live projects and internships at reputed universities. Thousands of students have attended, built and implemented AI solutions, and earned certificates. Students from prestigious universities have joined the internship program and completed it with genuine, applied knowledge.
