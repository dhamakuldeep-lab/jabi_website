Why this internship is different

Many internships hand out certificates for watching videos. Ours is built around a real project: interns do actual work, guided by a mentor with deep industry experience, and leave with something they can genuinely talk about in interviews - backed by a verifiable experience letter.

How it works

Work on a real AI project

Interns join a live project relevant to their field - healthcare, engineering, management or general AI.

1:1 mentorship

A mentor guides each intern, reviews their work, and helps them grow practical skills.

Real deliverables

Interns produce actual output - analysis, a model, a prototype - that demonstrates their ability.

Experience letter and certificate

On successful completion, interns receive a verifiable experience letter and certificate reflecting the real work they did.
