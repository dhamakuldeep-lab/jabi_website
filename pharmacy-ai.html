AI in Healthcare for Pharmacy Students

A practical program on how artificial intelligence is transforming pharma - from drug discovery to patient safety - taught by a founder who built healthcare AI platforms serving millions.

Book this Program

Why this program

AI is changing how medicines are discovered, tested and monitored. Pharmacy graduates who understand these tools have a clear edge in industry, research and clinical roles. This workshop introduces them in plain, practical terms - no prior coding needed - and is led by someone who has actually built and deployed healthcare AI at scale.

What we cover

AI in drug discovery

How machine learning speeds up identifying drug candidates, predicting molecular properties, and reducing trial-and-error in the lab.

Pharmacovigilance with machine learning

Using AI to detect adverse drug reactions and safety signals from large datasets, and how regulators adopt these methods.

Clinical data and patient care

How AI supports diagnosis, dosage optimisation and personalised medicine, with real examples from healthcare settings.

Hands-on project

Participants work on a guided health-data project - analysing a real dataset and presenting findings - so the learning sticks.

What participants achieve

  • Understand where AI fits across the pharma value chain
  • Work hands-on with a real health dataset
  • Build a project to showcase in interviews
  • Receive a certificate and a verifiable experience letter
