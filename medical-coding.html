Medical Coding & Billing

A career-focused training program in medical coding and billing - one of the fastest-growing healthcare back-office fields - open to pharmacy and any graduate or post-graduate.

Book this Program

Why this program

Medical coding and billing is a large, stable and growing industry serving healthcare providers worldwide. It offers strong career opportunities for graduates from pharmacy, life sciences, and any other background - no medical degree required. This program gives the practical, job-ready skills employers look for, taught by a team with deep US healthcare experience.

What we cover

Medical coding fundamentals

Understanding medical terminology, anatomy basics, and the major coding systems (such as ICD, CPT and HCPCS) used across the industry.

Medical billing & the revenue cycle

How claims, billing and the healthcare revenue cycle work end to end, and the role coders and billers play.

Accuracy, compliance & tools

Working accurately, understanding compliance, and using the software tools used in real coding/billing jobs.

Job-readiness

Practical exercises, case examples, and guidance to prepare for roles and certification exams in the field.

What participants achieve

  • Job-ready medical coding & billing skills
  • Understanding of the healthcare revenue cycle
  • Preparation for industry certification exams
  • A certificate and verifiable experience letter
