A practical, project-based AI program for engineering students across all branches - building real skills in machine learning and applied AI, taught by a founder who has shipped AI at scale.

Why this program

Every engineering branch now intersects with AI - from civil to mechanical to electronics to computer science. This workshop gives engineering students a strong, practical foundation in artificial intelligence and machine learning, with a hands-on project they can show employers.

What we cover

AI & machine learning foundations

Core concepts explained clearly - how AI models learn, the main types, and where each is used in engineering.

Hands-on with real AI tools

Students use industry-standard tools and frameworks to build and run their own models.

Applied AI for your branch

Examples and mini-projects mapped to different engineering disciplines, so the learning is relevant.

Capstone project

Each participant builds a working AI mini-project and presents it, ending with a certificate and experience letter.

What participants achieve

  • A solid, practical foundation in AI and ML
  • Hands-on experience with real AI tools
  • A working AI project for your portfolio
  • A certificate and verifiable experience letter
