The Founder

A career building AI that real people rely on.

Jabi Intelligents is founded by Kuldeep Dhama, who brings 26 years of experience in software and artificial intelligence in corporate leadership roles — including 15 years working onsite in the USA.

He holds a B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering and has completed an Executive Leadership Program at Harvard University (Boston, USA) — combining deep technical depth with global leadership training.

Across senior positions as Head of Research & Development (Data Science), Country Head and Chief Architect in software engineering, he designed and successfully implemented AI platforms and machine-learning models in healthcare. Those platforms are in production today, serving millions of members.

He started Jabi Intelligents to close the gap between how AI is taught and how it is actually built and used in industry — bringing that hands-on, real-world perspective directly to the next generation.

What we do

We partner with colleges, universities and companies to deliver practical AI education designed around each audience's field and goals. Every program is hands-on and ends with proof of the work — a certificate and a verifiable experience letter.

Our track record

Through Jabi Intelligents, we have organised several workshops at reputed universities. Thousands of students have attended, learned and implemented AI solutions, and earned certificates. Students from prestigious universities have joined our internship program and completed it with genuine, applied knowledge.

Why it matters

Too much AI training stops at theory. Because our programs are led by someone who has actually shipped AI at scale, students get the real picture — what works, what doesn't, and how to apply it. That's the difference between hearing about AI and being able to do it.
