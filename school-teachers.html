AI for School Teachers

A friendly, hands-on workshop that helps school teachers use AI in their daily work - planning lessons, creating materials, grading support and saving time - to boost productivity and efficiency.

Book this Program

Why this program

AI can save teachers hours every week - if they know how to use it well. This workshop is built for school teachers with no technical background, showing practical, safe and effective ways to bring AI into everyday teaching and admin work.

What we cover

AI for lesson planning & materials

Using AI tools to draft lesson plans, worksheets, quizzes and presentations in minutes.

Saving time on everyday tasks

Email, reports, feedback, summaries - practical ways AI reduces routine workload.

Making learning engaging

Using AI to create examples, activities and differentiated material for different students.

Using AI responsibly

Simple guidance on accuracy, privacy and safe, ethical use of AI in a school setting.

What participants achieve

  • Save hours each week on routine tasks
  • Create lessons and materials faster with AI
  • Use AI tools confidently and responsibly
  • A participation certificate for your records
