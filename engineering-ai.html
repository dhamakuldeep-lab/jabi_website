AI for Industry 4.0

A hands-on program connecting AI with the smart factory - IoT, automation and predictive maintenance - designed for engineering students, corporate teams, ITI and polytechnic learners.

Book this Program

Why this program

Industry 4.0 fuses physical machines with data and AI. Engineering graduates who can work at this intersection are in high demand across manufacturing, energy and automotive sectors. This workshop makes those concepts concrete and practical.

What we cover

Industrial IoT and sensors

How connected sensors capture machine data, and how that data becomes the foundation for intelligent systems.

Predictive maintenance

Building models that predict equipment failure before it happens - one of the highest-value uses of AI in industry.

Smart manufacturing and automation

How AI optimises production lines, quality control and supply chains in a modern factory.

Hands-on project

Participants build a factory-floor style project using sample sensor data, ending with a working demonstration.

What participants achieve

  • Understand the Industry 4.0 technology stack
  • Build a predictive-maintenance style model
  • Connect classroom theory to real factory use-cases
  • Receive a certificate and a verifiable experience letter
