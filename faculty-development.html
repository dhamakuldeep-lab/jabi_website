Why this program

Faculty are the multipliers of any skill - when teachers understand AI, hundreds of students benefit. This FDP gives educators both the concepts and the teaching tools to confidently integrate AI into their subjects and research.

What we cover

AI foundations for educators

Core AI and machine-learning concepts explained for teaching, with discipline-specific examples.

AI teaching toolkit

Ready-to-use teaching materials, demonstrations and assignments faculty can bring straight into class.

Research and publication support

How to identify AI research opportunities and strengthen publications.

Curriculum integration

Practical guidance on adding AI modules to existing courses.

What participants achieve

  • Confidence to teach AI concepts in your subject
  • A toolkit of teaching materials and demos
  • Ideas for AI-related research and publications
  • An FDP completion certificate
